Washing Machine Care Tips

Simple maintenance tips to keep your washer running smoothly.

1. Don’t overload the drum

Too much laundry causes bearing wear. Leave some space to prevent noise and damage.

2. Clean the filter regularly

If your washer doesn’t drain, check the filter — lint and small objects often block it.

3. Remove limescale once a month

Limescale on the heating element increases power use. Use descaler to extend machine life.

Why the washing machine does not drain water

In most cases, the reason is simple — a clogged filter or drain hose. If the problem remains, it may be the pump or control board. Call the master in Kyiv for diagnostics.
